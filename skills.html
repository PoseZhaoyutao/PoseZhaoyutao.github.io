Research Paper Writer

A research manuscript skill that turns session memory, experiment logs, figures, metrics, prior drafts, and code artifacts into evidence-grounded paper workflows.

  • Builds an auditable evidence ledger before manuscript claims are used.
  • Requires independent roles for writing, editorial review, domain review, and formatting checks.
  • Supports drafting, revision, review, and submission-oriented preparation for Markdown, LaTeX, Word, and PDF workflows.
